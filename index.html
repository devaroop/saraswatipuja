About Us

Who we are

We are a group of professionals from the Indian community who live in the beautiful county of Hertfordshire and its surroundings. We conceptualized the idea of doing Saraswati Puja from a small house in Watford in 2013 with a private celebration but an overwhelming response forced us to think beyond and in 2014 with a proper IDOL shipped from Kolkata whilst someone from us volunteering their garage, we hosted it successfully for the public from that small place. The response that we got was amazing hence again it forced us to shift the venue to a larger place to accommodate more people in 2015. We saw Indians in Watford enthusiastically came together to celebrate " SARASWATI PUJA @ WATFORD ", with an aim to:

1. Provide a forum to converge and celebrate our unique cultural, redivgious, artistic and social heritage

2. Promote multiculturadivsm to enhance our experience through a multitude of activities

3. Generate greater awareness about Indian culture within the youth and encourage their involvement

That was the beginning and it’s been now 3 yrs in a row that we have taken our first steps. We have been touched and motivated by the enthusiasm and support that we have received from our local community. We strongly believe and sincerely hope that this will turn into a focal point where the community can come together and enjoy the true spirit of our rich culture.

We are looking forward with excitement to work more closely with our local community here and Our goal is to contribute towards the many good causes and challenges that face our society today.
